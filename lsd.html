LSD [Acid, Blotters] ~Psychedelic~

Background: Created by Albert Hofmann in 1938, popular as a recreational drug by the 1960s.

Effects & Risks:

  • Euphoria, music enhancement, anxiety (especially at higher doses), visual hallucinations, insomnia, pupil dilation, nausea
  • Long Term: Cognitive and mental health issues (somewhat rare)

Duration: 8 ~ 12hrs

Comeup: 30 ~ 90mins

Peak: 2 ~ 5hrs

Plateau: 5 ~ 8hrs

Comedown: 8 ~ 12hrs

Doses:

  • Threshold: 40 ~ 75µg
  • Standard: 75 ~ 150µg
  • High: 200 ~ 400µg
  • Heroic: 400+µg

*Doses depend on tolerance*

Other Information:

  • LSD is a very potent psychedelic, known for its stimulating effects and visual hallucinations.
  • Higher and heroic doses can be risky due to the mental intensity of the trip.
  • OTHER DRUGS ARE RARE TO FIND IN LSD BUT THERE HAVE BEEN INSTANCES OF OVERDOSE ON 25I-NBOMBe BEING MISLABELLED. IF YOUR LSD TASTES LIKE ANYTHING, DON'T TAKE IT.

Substance Mixing:

Dangerous/Avoid:

  • Some antidepressants, especially SSRIs are dangerous to mix with LSD due to the risk of serotonin syndrome.
  • Most other stimulating drugs should not be mixed with LSD due to the cardiovascular stress it causes.

Safe/Caution:

  • Cannabis products can be safely mixed with LSD, with the risk of paranoia or intensity.
  • Other psychedelics are generally safe to mix with LSD, but higher doses may cause overwhelming effects.
  • Empathogens like MDMA can be compatible if taken cautiously.
