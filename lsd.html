LSD [Acid, Blotters] ~Psychedelic~

Background

Created by Albert Hofmann in 1938, popular as a recreational drug by the 1960s.

Effects & Risks

  • Euphoria
  • Music enhancement
  • Anxiety (especially at higher doses)
  • Visual hallucinations
  • Insomnia
  • Pupil dilation
  • Nausea

Long Term: Cognitive and mental health issues (somewhat rare)

Duration

8 ~ 12hrs

  • Comeup: 30 ~ 90mins
  • Peak: 2 ~ 5hrs
  • Plateau: 5 ~ 8hrs
  • Comedown: 8 ~ 12hrs

Doses

  • Threshold: 40 ~ 75µg
  • Standard: 75 ~ 150µg
  • High: 200 ~ 400µg
  • Heroic: 400+µg

*Doses depend on tolerance*

Other Information

LSD is a very potent psychedelic, known for its stimulating effects and visual hallucinations. It is very commonly taken at parties or raves due to its music enhancement effects. It got popular in the 1960s through its association with the counterculture movement of the time and eventually media talked positively and negatively about it. It's usually consumed on designed paper tabs about ¼" in size. Some people use liquid LSD through droppers as well but it's less common. Higher and Heroic doses can be risky due to the mental intensity of the trip, with things like bad trips, extreme confusion, and loss of self and reality.

**OTHER DRUGS ARE RARE TO FIND IN LSD BUT THERE HAVE BEEN INSTANCES OF OVERDOSE ON 25I-NBOMBe BEING MISLABELLED. IF YOUR LSD TASTES LIKE ANYTHING, DON'T TAKE IT.**

Substance Mixing

Dangerous/Avoid:

  • Some antidepressants, especially SSRIs (risk of serotonin syndrome)
    Examples: Fluoxetine, Sertraline, Venlafaxine
  • Stimulants (risk of cardiovascular stress or fatality)
    Examples: Cocaine, Amphetamines (Adderall, Meth), high doses of Caffeine

Safe/Caution:

  • Cannabis products (may intensify experience or calm it)
    Examples: Weed
  • Other psychedelics (safe but increased chance of overwhelming experience)
    Examples: Psilocybin Mushrooms, 2C-B, Peyote
  • Empathogens (compatible but caution needed with dose)
    Examples: MDMA, MDA, 6-APB, Methylone
  • Dissociatives (low/moderate doses generally okay; high doses risky)
    Examples: DXM, PCP, high dose Ketamine
