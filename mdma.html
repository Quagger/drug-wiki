MDMA (Molly)

Home | LSD | MDMA

Background

Created in 1912, popular as a street drug by the 1980s.

Effects & Risks

Effects:

  • Intense euphoria, stimulation, increased sociability, insomnia, nausea, jaw clenching, pupil dilation, serotonin syndrome
  • Long Term: Cognitive and mental health issues, serotonin depletion

Fatal Overdose Risk: Low

Duration

Duration: 3 ~ 6hrs

Onset: 30 ~ 60mins

Peak: 1.5 ~ 3hrs

Comedown: 3 ~ 6hrs

Doses

  • Threshold: 30 ~ 50mg
  • Standard: 75 ~ 125mg
  • High: 125 ~ 160mg
  • Redosing: 40 ~ 60mg (Only redose after 1.5 - 2hrs.)

Note: Increases neurotoxicity ~ Decreases euphoria

Dosing depends on body weight and tolerance

Other Information

MDMA is a powerful stimulant that usually makes people want to socialize, making it a popular choice as a party drug. MDMA comes in different forms, with pills and powder being the most common. Pills are orally ingested, and powder is often insufflated. While rolling, you may feel hot or dehydrated, so it's essential to drink water but not too much. Comedowns are usually difficult and result in a hard depressive crash. MDMA releases large amounts of serotonin, so it is recommended to wait 4 ~ 6+ weeks before using Molly again to maintain healthy serotonin levels.

WARNING: MDMA is often unpure and may contain other substances like opioids or methamphetamine.

Substance Mixing

Dangerous/Avoid:

  • Most antidepressants (especially SSRIs) due to serotonin syndrome risk
  • Other stimulating drugs (Cocaine, Amphetamines, etc.)
  • Empathogens like MDA

Safe/Caution:

  • Cannabis products
  • Psychedelics (LSD, Psilocybin, 2C-B, etc.)
  • Benzodiazepines (Xanax, Ativan, etc.)
