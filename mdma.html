MDMA (Molly)

Home | LSD | MDMA

~ Empathogen ~

Other Names: MDMA, Ecstasy, Molly

Background: Created in 1912, became popular as a street drug by the 1980s.

Effects & Risks

  • Intense euphoria
  • Stimulation
  • Increased sociability
  • Insomnia, nausea, jaw clenching, pupil dilation
  • Serotonin syndrome (risk when combined with other drugs)
  • Long Term: Cognitive and mental health issues, serotonin depletion

Fatal Overdose Risk: Low

Duration

  • Duration: 3 – 6 hours
  • Onset: 30 – 60 minutes
  • Peak: 1.5 – 3 hours
  • Comedown: 3 – 6 hours

Doses

  • Threshold: 30 – 50mg
  • Standard: 75 – 125mg
  • High: 125 – 160mg
  • Redosing: 40 – 60mg (Only redose after 1.5–2 hours)

*Redosing increases neurotoxicity and decreases euphoria*

*Dosing depends on body weight and tolerance*

Other Information

MDMA is a powerful stimulant that increases empathy and sociability, making it a common party drug.

Comes in pill or powder form; pills are ingested, powder is often insufflated (snorted).

While rolling, users may feel hot or dehydrated — stay hydrated, but avoid excessive water intake.

Comedowns can be harsh and depressive. Since MDMA depletes serotonin, users should wait 4–6+ weeks before using again.

WARNING: MDMA is often unpure and may contain harmful substances like opioids or methamphetamine.

Substance Mixing

Dangerous / Avoid

  • Antidepressants: Can block MDMA or cause serotonin syndrome (potentially fatal).
    Examples: Fluoxetine, Sertraline, Venlafaxine
  • Other stimulants: Add heart strain and risk of fatality.
    Examples: Cocaine, Adderall, Meth, high-dose caffeine
  • Other empathogens: High neurotoxicity risk, potential serotonin syndrome.
    Examples: MDA, 6-APB, MBDB, Ethylone

Safe / Caution

  • Cannabis: Usually safe, but may increase intensity or paranoia.
    Example: Weed
  • Psychedelics: No toxic interaction, but may be overwhelming. Use with caution and experience.
    Examples: LSD, Psilocybin, 2C-B, Peyote
  • Benzodiazepines: Safe and often used for comedown or anxiety, not for enhancing the roll.
    Examples: Xanax, Ativan
